December 18, 2021

Dear Applicant,

We have completed our Early Action review of your application and have chosen to defer it to our Regular Action review. Please know that the Admissions Committee is very conservative in its early offers of admission, and the majority of students who apply in Early Action are deferred. In previous years, several hundred of them have subsequently been admitted in Regular Action.

Please remember to complete the February Updates and Notes Form, which will be available through your account in mid-January. The form allows you to share your midyear grades and provides space for other updates to your application. Updates are not required, but if there is something you want to share, this is the opportunity to do so. You will hear from us again at the end of Regular Action in March after further review of your application.

We hope that you have a healthy—and safe—holiday season, and best wishes for a happy New Year.


Sincerely,

Stuart Schmill
Dean of Admissions and Student Financial Services
